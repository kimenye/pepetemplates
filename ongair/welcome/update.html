The Growth of instant messaging

Our primary platform WhatsApp announced it has 700 million active users as well as handling 30B messages daily. By signing up for Ongair you have taken the first step in ensuring that your brand is part of the conversation.

Recent Updates from WhatsApp

WhatsApp has been in the news a lot lately. From the introduction of end-to-end encryption in November 2014 to the recent launch of their WhatsApp on Web solution. We were quick to implement encryption in order to improve the security of your messages. The WhatsApp on Web solution is targeting personal users who want to be able to receive and respond to their personal computers. This is an exciting prospect for managing your personal WhatsApp account, however it is a little thin as far as corporates are concerned. Most prominently:

  • It only allows one user at a time to manage the WhatsApp number
  • It does not offer the advanced searching, contact management and reporting we provide for Ongair.

With Ongair you will continue to enjoy these and many more features.

Temporary Bans

WhatsApp has also announced a crackdown on unofficial clients connecting to WhatsApp. This is an ongoing practice that dates back to 2012. Rest assured that we are operating within the WhatsApp terms and conditions and do not run the risk of getting banned.
