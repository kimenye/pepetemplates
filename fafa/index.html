Dear Friends

This year's FAFA and Fashion for Peace is just round the corner. I really hope you can come & bring friends and colleagues with you.

Fashion for Peace was initiated in 2008 out of a need to bring people together and to show the beautiful side of Africa after they had been bombarded with images of violence. Again this year the same motives are very important, both locally and internationally. We need to get our positive energy back and we want to use creativity to do that.

The Saturday night gala will be a wonderful evening to feel great, dress up to the nines, and celebrate life. A chance to mix with those you know, and reach out to others, while enjoying fabulous cocktails from Jameson and great African food by Sarova Stanley.

Sunday will show yet more amazing creations as well as an opportunity to buy the latest looks. While you are shopping your teenagers can learn exciting creative arts like photography, designing, graffiti art, dj-ing, doing a haircut etc from the very best professionals.
