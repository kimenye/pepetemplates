What is mfukoni by Chase Bank?

Mfukoni is a mobile banking application that lets you access banking services from your mobile phones & tablets; anytime, anywhere. It's also available on USSD.

How do I get started?

  1. Download the Chase Mfukoni App from any of the App stores (Google Stores, Blackberry Store, Apple Store, Ovi Store etc)
  2. Dial *275# from Safaricom, Airtel
  3. Click the Self Register Option and Sign Up or Visit your nearest Chase Bank branch.
  4. Login details will be sent to your phone
  5. You will be prompted to change the PIN upon first successful login

NB: Every account is unique to every mobile number when being linked.
