Welcome to Mfukoni.

A mobile banking application specially designed to enable you to meet your financial needs and access your bank account in a more convenient and efficient way. Making your life Simply Apptastic.

Ways to access Mfukoni.

Accessible from your mobile phone or tab anywhere, anytime.

  1. Dial *275#. Reply with your PIN.
  2. If internet enabled, download the Mfukoni App from your respective App store.
  3. Open the App and log in using your valid username and password to access available services.

Platforms Supported by Mfukoni.

  1. Android OS (Android Phone & Android Tablet)
  2. Blackberry OS
  3. iOS (iPhone and iPad)
  4. Symbian OS (Nokia & Java phones)
  5. Windows Mobile
  6. USSD - Dial *275#
